SPECIFICATIONS

The Personal Brewery is designed to fit on your kitchen countertop and plug into a typical 15 amp home socket.

Yield: 2 gallons of beer (~18 bottles of beer)
Size: 17” X 17” X 22”
Power: 1,575 Watts
Materials: 304 stainless steel, silicone, acetol copolymer, high temp ryton, Teflon, nickel-chromium alloy
Microcontroller: Netdruino Plus 2
